Terrain Effects on Combat
🏙️ Major City — Defender CF ×2
⛰️ Mountain — Def ×2 · Non-mtn units stop
🌊 River — Def ×2 if all attackers on river side
🌲 Woods — Units stop (inf/mtn/para/LW inf exempt)
  Attackers can't retreat on AR/A1 · Defenders on DR/EX/D1
🌿 Swamp — Units stop · Clear terrain in Snow
⚓ Kerch Strait — Stop 1st hex · Def ×2
Supply Rules
• Max 8 hexes to city or friendly rail hex
• 4 hexes maximum in Snow turns
• Line cannot cross EZOC or enemy-controlled city
• Out of supply at End Phase → eliminated
❄️ Winter Combat Supply (1941–42 + 1942–43 winters):
Axis must be in/adjacent to Axis city for full CF
Without combat supply: CF halved (round up per unit)

Campaign Victory Conditions
Axis wins immediately if:
• Controls Moscow AND Stalin eliminated
• Russia doesn't control Berlin at end of May/Jun 1945
• Russia can't match Axis units exited west board edge
Soviet wins immediately if:
• Controls Berlin at any point
• Axis can't match Soviet units exited east board edge
